My journey


My journey started on a farm in the Northern Cape province. How did this farm girl became a Data Scientist.... read on!

My journey continued, after matriculating, studying B.Sc. in the field of Agricultural Economics at the University of the Free State.
I embarked my Agricultural Economic career with the Department of Agriculture in Bloemfontein. Here I dived into the world of economics, markets, prices and projects. I completed project feasability studies and project management for small scale agricultural projects.
We hosted farmer days to help upcomming farmers how to identify their target markets and how to deliver their products to their customers.
These projects broaden my knowledge and skills for project management, market research and analysis, gathering and analyzing of data.

After this, life happend, took me on a different path, one that led to Data Science and Hyperiondev. I learned my first programming language -- Python. Then I moved on to Machine learning and Deep Learning.
Currently I am doing the Software Engineering Bootcamp. The biggest surprise for me was....I love this!!
